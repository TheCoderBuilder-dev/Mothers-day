A Letter Just For You

May 11, 2025

Dearest Mom,

As Mother's Day comes around again, I find myself reflecting on the countless ways you've shaped my life and filled it with love. Words seem inadequate to express the depth of my gratitude, but I'll try anyway.

You've been my rock during storms, my cheerleader during victories, my counselor during confusion, and my comfort during pain. Your strength and grace under pressure have taught me more than any lesson could about resilience and dignity.

I remember how you never missed a performance, how you always knew when I needed a gentle push forward. These moments might have seemed small to you, but they were everything to me.

Your wisdom guides me still, your voice of reason echoes in my mind when I face difficult choices. The values you instilled – kindness, honesty, perseverance – form the foundation of who I am today.

Thank you for believing in me, especially when I didn't believe in myself. Thank you for sacrificing so much to give me opportunities. Thank you for loving me unconditionally, through all my mistakes and triumphs.

Today and always, I celebrate you – not just as my mother, but as the extraordinary woman you are.

With all my love,

Brian a.k.a. Kaman
